title: Following a routine makes time moves faster
type: shorts
date: June 7, 2021
---

Doing the same things over and over again everyday like working 9-5 alters your perception towards time as in making it seems to move faster.

This is due to the less unique moments have been created in your memory at a given period especially with the same environment.

Everyone could agree that 2020 moves so fast, the fastest year ever thanks to the lockdown and travel restrictions.
